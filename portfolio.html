NissenERT

Project Overview

Dan first approached Brady Nissen (owner of NissenERT) in early 2017 to inquire about creating his business website. The goal with his website was to establish an online presence, attract customers, and bring awareness to his business in the Des Moines Area. NissenERT's design is a content first approach to differentiate itself from its competition which would be to less busy and more informative.

Technologies Used:

Objectives Achieved:

  • Established an online presence.
  • Established social media presence.
  • Developed a contact form from customers wanting more information or to sign up for classes.
  • Implemented search engine optimizations to help improve search results in the Des Moines Area.
  • Informative frequently asked questions, where it overviews the importance of emergency response training.
  • Optimized using Gzip compression.
