Crockpot Chili

  • Serves 6
  • Preparation: 25 minutes
  • Cooking: 6 hours
  • Level: Medium
  • Source: School Sample Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 cup onion
  • 1 cup chopped peppers
  • 4 tablespoon Chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon Hot chili powder (optional)
  • 1 lb. ground beef or chicken
  • 2 cans Red Beans
  • 2 cans Kidney Beans
  • 2 cans Tomato Puree
  • 2 cans Tomato Sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (optional)
  • 1/2 cup sour cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat cooking oil in 2 quart
  2. Saute onions and peppers for 5 minutes
  3. Add spices and stir for 30 seconds.
  4. Add meat and cook until browned. Approximately 15 minutes.
  5. Pour contents of skillet into 3 quart crock pot.
  6. Rinse beans and place in crockpot.
  7. Open and pour Tomato puree and sauce into crock pot.
  8. Cover crockpot and cook on low for 6 hours.
  9. Serve into individual bowls and top with sour cream and cheese.

Devilish Chili-Cheese Dogs

  • Serves 4 (2 dogs each)
  • Prep: 15 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes
  • Total: 30 minutes
  • Level: Easy
  • Source: Food Network

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil, 1 turn of the pan
  • 1 pound ground sirloin
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire Sauce -- eyeball it
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder, a palmful
  • 1 (8-ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 8 fat or foot-long beef franks
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon hot sauce
  • 8 hot dog buns, toasted
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar, 1 (10-ounce sack)

Instructions

  1. Heat a skillet over medium high heat.
  2. Add extra-virgin olive oil and meat and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Brown and crumble beef.
  4. Add Worcestershire, onion, garlic, chili powder and cook together 5 minutes.
  5. Add tomato sauce and reduce heat to low.
  6. Meanwhile, boil franks in shallow pan to warm through, 5 minutes.
  7. Drain water and return pan to medium heat.
  8. Score casings on dogs.
  9. Melt butter in skillet and hot sauce.
  10. Add dogs to pan, browning and crisping the casings in hot sauce and butter.
  11. Heat broiler.
  12. Place devilish dogs in buns and top with chili and lots of cheese.
  13. Place devilish dogs under broiler and melt cheese.
  14. Serve immediately.

Chili Cheese Fries

  • Serves: 6
  • Prep: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 20 minutes
  • Total: 25 minutes
  • Level: Easy
  • Source: AllRecipes - Mike

Ingredients

  • 1 (32 ounce) package frozen seasoned french fries
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 cups low-fat milk
  • 1 tablespoon margarine
  • 8 slices American cheese, cut into pieces
  • 1 (15 ounce) can chili without beans (such as HormelÂ®)

Instructions

  1. Prepare french fries as directed on the package
  2. Stir cornstarch and water in a small cup until cornstarch dissolves; set aside.
  3. Bring milk and margarine to a boil in a saucepan, stirring constantly.
  4. Reduce the heat and whisk the cornstarch mixture into the milk mixture, bring to a simmer over medium heat.
  5. Cook and stir until the mixture is thick and smooth.
  6. Add the cheese to the milk mixture and stir until the cheese has melted and is well combined.
  7. Prepare chili as directed on the can.
  8. Pour the cooked chili and the cheese sauce over the top of the cooked french fries.
